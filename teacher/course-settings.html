    Course Points

    Course points indicate student progress in a single number. Points are typically rewarded for direct course mastering such as task completion. The final grade for the course is commonly based on course points.

    Student can always see his/her course points and leaderboards are always accessable for a teacher.
    With public leaderboard any student can see each other's course points.

What is Gamification?

Gamification is the use of game thinking and game mechanics in non-game contexts to engage users in solving problems. Gamification techniques strive to leverage people's natural desires for socializing, learning, mastery, competition, achievement, status, self-expression, altruism, or closure.
