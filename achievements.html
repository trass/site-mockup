Achievement types

  • Bronze Achievement
    Bronze achievements encourage students to try out ways to approach study. They are easy to get if you try!
  • Silver Achievement
    Silver achievements are less common than bronze ones. Student will need to work harder get one of these.
  • Gold Achievement
    Gold achievements mark outstanding student performance. They are rarely awarded.
  • Secret Achievement
    Secret achievements are meant to be awarded for the original and/or funny actions. Each secret achievement description is hidden from each student until the latter gets awarded with the former.
